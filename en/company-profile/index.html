Company Profile & History About Us

EMIRATES GLOBAL FUNDING PJSC. (the “Company”) is a private joint stock company incorporated in the Emirate of Dubai, United Arab Emirates, in accordance with the provisions of the U.A.E. Federal Commercial Companies Law No. 8 of 1984, (as amended), the U.A.E. Central Bank, the Monetary System and Organization of Banking Law No. (10) of 1980 and under authority of resolutions of the Board of Directors of the U.A.E Central Bank relating to Finance Companies.

EMIRATES GLOBAL FUNDING PJSC. Is based in Dubai and is licensed and regulated by the UAE Central Bank, with a paid up capital base of AED 960,000,000,000 Billion.

The company was incorporated on 15th March 2008 and has its registered offices at Downtown Dubai, Near Burj Khalifa - Dubai - United Arab Emirates.
