Board of Directors

Sheikh Tariq bin Faisal Al Qassimi

Chairman – EMIRATES GLOBAL FUNDING PJSC

Sheikh Tariq bin Faisal Al Qassimi is a member of the Ruling Family of the Emirate of Sharjah, United Arab Emirates. As a former member of the Executive Council of the Government of Sharjah he led the Economic Committee for five years. His business interests include investments, real estate, property development, trading, construction, insurance, finance, transportation, contracting, services, and hospitality.

Sheikh Tariq is an active investor in capital markets, holding a diversified portfolio of investments in local and GCC equity markets, as well as emerging and developed markets, with additional investments in private equity funds, hedge funds, Global REITs and commodity funds.  

As Chairman of EMIRATES GLOBAL FUNDING PJSC, Sheikh Tariq oversees a diversified investment operation with major interests in various sectors. In that capacity, a leading developer of premium-quality commercial, residential and hospitality projects in the UAE, Pakistan and Australia with interests in Palazzo Versace Gold Coast and Dubai; a general business aviation company involved in sales, charter and management of executive aircraft; Emirates Tarian Capital, including real estate management and luxury property development over 25 branches across the country.

Before focusing on his private business interests, Sheikh Tariq was heavily involved in the development of Sharjah, beginning in 1999 with his appointment as Chairman for the General Authority of Sharjah Free Zones. He has also held important posts as Chairman of the Sharjah Economic Development Department (SEDD) and the Sharjah Commerce and Tourism Development Authority (SCTDA), and has chaired government institutions responsible for Awqaf, sports and charity.

Academic qualifications:

    Master’s degree in International Finance, Westminster University, UK. He graduated from the Higher Colleges of Technology, UAE, Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Higher Colleges of Technology, UAE.
    Executive Management Certificate, Harvard University.
    Sheikh Tariq has received additional training and certification from the London Business School, Stanford University and the University of Cambridge.





Dr. Khaled Miklad

Director – EMIRATES GLOBAL FUNDING PJSC

Dr. Khaled Miklad is the Business development Manager, Sharjah’s primary SME funding agency. He is also the Business Development Manager for Future Leaders Training & Consultancy Center which aims to prepare leaders for both the government and private sectors. He is also Founding member in the Arab Union for small businesses affiliated to the Arab League.

His career path includes work at the Dubai department of Economic development, the Mohammed Bin Rashid Foundation for the Support of Business Leaders, the Ajman Chamber of Commerce & Industry as well as work at the office of the Prime Minister of Egypt and the Minister of the Public Enterprise Sector prior to his arrival in the UAE.

Ghanem Mubarak Al Hajeri

Advocate, Founding Partner

Qualification

Ghanem Mubarak Al Hajeri is graduated with a Bachelor of Shariah & Law from Emirates University in Abu Dhabi and is advocate admitted to practice before UAE Courts of First Instance and Courts of Appeal.

Professional Experience

In addition to other legal expertise, Ghanem has vast experience in the field of conciliations and commercial arbitration. He worked as Conciliation & Arbitration Advisor in Abu Dhabi Commercial Conciliation & Arbitration Centre, as Executive Manager at the commercial Court of First Instance in Abu Dhabi in 2009 and as Notary Public at Abu Dhabi Judicial Department. Ghanem has been approved as qualified “arbitrator” by the GCC Commercial Arbitration Centre and also by Sharjah International Arbitration Centre.

Specialties

International Business Law, Corporate & Commercial Law, Arbitration, Property and Rental Disputes, IP/IT
